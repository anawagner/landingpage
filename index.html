What WE do

Process Modeling

W.E. has decades of experience creating finite element models of manufacturing processes used to produce aerospace parts and assemblies. Let us know if you have a need to solve a manufacturing issue or want to be certain the parts will be made accurately before buying any material.

Material characterization, and other services required for creating accurate models, can be performed for any new materials you want to use in your application.

Process Improvement

W.E. use proven methods of continuous improvement to reduce costs and eliminate waste in manufacturing processes. Contact us to discuss how lean and six sigma methods can be implemented to improve your processes.

Project Management

W.E. employs PMI certified Project Management Professionals and uses the project management body of knowledge that is used to effectively manage successful projects all over the world. W.E. would be happy to help you if you are at the point where you're ready to write a charter or need to plan for the celebration for you projects success. W.E. will make sure that the lessons learned are available for any future projects.
